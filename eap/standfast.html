Standfast

Standfast is used for a developing event that has potential to escalate or limit Volunteer movement due to increased risk, or impact Post operations. During Standfast Volunteers remain at their sites (or current location) and should not travel.

Examples of Standfast situations:
• Terrorist attack that occurs in an area where Post operates
• A significant or large-scale attack started at the line of conflict
• Planned or unplanned protests with some violence expected or reported
• Explosions may affect PCVs sites
• Serious incident took place at the nuclear power plant, details are being clarified
• Number of disease cases keeps growing, the risk of infection during travel and/or interaction with large number of people has significantly increased
