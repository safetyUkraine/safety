Agencies

Agencies dealing with emergencies in Ukraine are the following:

State Emergency Service:
The State Emergency Service of Ukraine (SESU) is a central body of executive power with activities directed and coordinated by the Cabinet of Ministers through the Minister of internal affairs. It implements state policies in the domain of civil protection, protection of population and territories from emergencies, prevention of emergencies, rectification of emergency consequences, rescue work, fire extinguishing, fire and technogenic safety, accident rescue service activities and hydrometeorologic activities.

In its activities, SESU abides by the Constitution and laws of Ukraine, edicts of the President of Ukraine and bylaws of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, enacted in accordance with the Constitution and laws of Ukraine, acts of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, and other acts of legislation.

The principal tasks of SESU are:
1) implementing state policies in the domain of civil protection, protection of population and territories from emergencies, prevention of emergencies, rectification of emergency consequences, rescue work, fire extinguishing, fire and technogenic safety, accident rescue service activities and hydrometeorologic activities;
2) providing state supervision (control) over observing and satisfying legal requirements in the domain of civil protection, fire and technogenic safety, accident rescue service activities;
3) introducing proposals on state policymaking in the aforesaid spheres for consideration by the Minister of internal affairs;
4) implementation, within its powers envisaged by the law, of state policies in volunteer activities.

National Police:
The National Police of Ukraine, established in August 2015, is a central executive body and directed and coordinated by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine through the Minister of Internal Affairs in accordance with the law.

The tasks of the National Police are to provide police services in the following areas:
1) public safety and public order;
2) security and protection of human rights and freedoms, as well as the interests of society and the state;
3) crime prevention;
4) provision of assistance services to persons who, for personal, economic, social reasons or as a result of emergencies need such assistance within the limits defined by law.

National Guard:
The National Guard of Ukraine (NSU) is a military formation with law enforcement functions. It is a part of the system of Ministry of Interior of Ukraine and is designed to fulfil the tasks related to protection and safeguarding the life, rights, freedoms and legitimate interests of citizens, society and the state from criminal and other wrongful infringements, and also maintenance of public order and public security, as well as to ensure, in coordination with law enforcement agencies, the national security and protection of national borders, stopping terrorist activities and activities of illegitimate paramilitary or armed troops (groups), terrorist organizations, organized groups and criminal organizations.

The uniqueness of the National Guard of Ukraine is in the fact that it performs law enforcement functions during the peacetime, stabilization tasks at the crisis period and military functions during the period of martial law.

Ministry of Ecology:
Ministry of Ecology and Natural resources operates in the field of environmental protection, ecological safety, treatment of waste, hazardous chemicals, pesticides and agricultural chemicals and perform state ecological expertise.

The Ministry is entitled in the area of atmospheric air, preservation of ozone layer, restoration and protection of flora and fauna, restoration and protection of lands, restoration and protection of water resources (surface, ground, sea waters), efficient usage of water resources.

Ministry of Health:
- Emergency prevention control
- Organization and monitoring and forecasting of emergencies and their development, determination of risks of their occurrence
- Providing affordable, free, timely and high-quality emergency medical care to victims of emergencies and elimination of their consequences
- Deployment of the sorting site and medical sorting of victims
- Epidemiological supervision of the condition of the sick, indicators of human life environment and appropriate laboratory monitoring in order to predict and prevent emergencies
- Ensuring medical protection of the population
- Creation, rational preservation and use of the reserve of material resources and financial resources necessary for the prevention and response to emergencies.

Ministry of Defense:
- Participation in emergency rescue and restoration works in case of particularly severe emergencies of anthropogenic and natural nature (natural disasters, catastrophes, especially large fires, pandemics, use of means of destruction, etc.), which pose a threat to the life and health of significant segments of the population
- Conservation and rational use of reserves of material resources necessary for the prevention and response to emergences and elimination of their consequences
- Ensuring observation, hydrometeorological forecasting and monitoring of the environment and hazardous processes that can lead to emergencies at potentially dangerous objects and objects of increased danger in subordinate organizations, in subordinate objects and adjacent territories.
