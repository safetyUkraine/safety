Civil Defense

Civil defense in Ukraine may be activated under following circusmstances:
1. Normal Circumstances
2. High Alert
3. Emergency Situation
4. Emergency State

Civil Defense structure:
1. Rescue services
2. Emergency services
3. Special services
4. Fire brigades
5. Volunteer formations


Tasks of Civil Defense:

1) ensuring readiness of ministries and other central and local executive authorities, local self-government bodies, their subordinate forces and means of action aimed at preventing and responding to emergencies;

2) ensuring the implementation of measures to prevent emergencies;

3) educating the population about behavior and actions in case of an emergency;

4) implementation of state target programs aimed at preventing emergencies, ensuring the sustainable functioning of enterprises, institutions and organizations, reducing possible material losses;

5) processing of information on emergencies, publication of information materials on the protection of the population and territories from the consequences of emergencies;

6) forecasting and assessing the socio-economic consequences of emergencies, determining on the basis of the forecast the need for forces, means, material and financial resources;

7) creation, rational preservation and use of the reserve of material and financial resources necessary for the prevention and response to emergencies;

8) notification of the population about the threat and emergencies, timely and reliable informing about the actual situation and measures taken;

9) protection of the population in case of emergencies;

10) carrying out rescue and other urgent work on the elimination of the consequences of emergencies, organization of life support of the affected population;

11) mitigation of possible consequences of emergencies in case of their occurrence;

12) implementation of measures for social protection of the affected population

Depending on the nature of the origin of events that may cause emergencies on the territory of Ukraine, the following types of emergencies are determined:
1) technological incidents;
2) natural disasters;
3) social;
4) military.

Depending on the amount of consequences caused by an emergency, the volumes of technical and material resources necessary for their elimination, following levels of emergencies are determined:
1) state;
2) regional;
3) local;
4) by objects.
