Alert

Alert is used for an event that has the potential to impact normal operations and warrants monitoring. During an Alert details of a potential threat are communicated to Volunteers as they are reminded to be vigilant and available for further communications. Other actions may include ongoing messaging, accountability checks or restricting travel to a specific area.

Examples of situation, when alert can be declared:
• General (not specific, not credible) warning about terrorism put out by the Embassy
• Information about movement of enemy’s forces along the border received
• Upcoming elections with no signs of unrest
• Demonstrations that may impact movement but are not foreseen to become violent
• A fire or an accident at military depot is reported, which is not considered a safety threat
• A fire or an accident at nuclear power plant is reported, radiation level is not exceeded
• Few suspected or confirmed cases of the Epidemic disease at PCV site/city/oblast
